Hi, I am Daniel Lee

And I am a Software Developer!

Optimism. Initiative. Integrity.

Daniel is an aspiring developer, with experience leading and developing various projects in school and personal spaces. His interests in computer science, visual design, music, and sports inform him mindful but competitive approach to the way he develops applications. He is fueled by his passion for helping people in needs of technology and education. Daniel considers himself a ‘humble learner’, always eager to learn new things and gives what he receives back to the community.

His hunger for knowledge and determination to turn information into action has contributed to his most recent position at Canadian Tire Corporation, where he analyzed the performance of internal applications to provide answers to unknown issues, and developed a license monitoring alert to help the company avoid unwanted penalties for license overages. Meanwhile, he improved the productivity of his team by assisting developers with testing automation scripts, processing tickets and managing several mini projects such as alert and dashboard naming standardization and managing KPI reports.

Daniel believes integrity in the workplace is key to success – listening to advices, developing good habits and executing new ideas. Daniel is currently in his final year of Computer Science degree program at Simon Fraser University in Vancouver, BC and is ready to embrace a new challenge.
